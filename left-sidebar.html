General Design Description

Facilities provided to the patients

Features

The Medical Billing Software is set up to be extremely user friendly to the Patient. It is set up in a manner that should be familiar to the Patient while offering a myriad of time saving features and devices. The different sections of the Medical Billing Software Electronic Medical Record are listed here in the order in which they appear in the system. All of the sections are provided on one page so that the Patient doesn't have to switch tabs.

Patients are registered at the reception and are seen on first come, first serve basis, however out of turn consultation may be provided in case of emergency and senior citizen. Patients have the right to consult any doctor. In OPD, clinical consultation is provided to patients which includes history taking, clinical examination, diagnosis and providing prescription to patients besides advising laboratory tests in some cases
An experienced Dental surgeon provides procedures like Dental Extractions, RCT, Scaling /Cleaning, Fillings, Local curettage
Ward facilities for observation and management of medical problem like typhoid, acute gastroenteritis, COPD, bronchial asthma, malaria, viral fever, pneumonias etc. There are 3 wards, one special room and one well equipped emergency.

Trained laboratory staff are providing best services which includes painless blood withdrawal Services of one NABL accredited laboratory are also available for carrying out specialised tests, they collect sample from 8 AM to 12 noon. In IIT Hospital. Sample collection time for IIT laboratory is 8 am to 10.30 am while emergency tests like Blood sugar, platelet count & Hb & blood grouping done in emergent cases throughout OPD hours.
24 hours patient transport vehicle available. Patients are transported from home (on campus) to IIT hospital and patients referred by emergency duty doctor to empanelled hospital for specialized case.
